Sunglass Emoji

The smiling face with sunglasses emoji is used to convey someone or something is cool, chill, easy-going, or carefree, sometimes with a bit of a snarky tone. It's also used to express appreciation or approval and to stand in for the sentiments of OK or awesome.

Thinking Emoji

The thinking face emoji can be used to reference a question or an idea. It also has a rhetorical use as a way to passive aggressively doubt someone or something.
